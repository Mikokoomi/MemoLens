# MemoLens - Private Beta Plan

## 1. Muc tieu private beta

Private beta giup mot nhom nho nguoi dung tin cay thu MemoLens voi hanh vi thuc te:

- Dang ky/dang nhap.
- Tao ky niem co anh.
- Upload nhieu anh hon demo.
- Dung timeline/search/filter.
- Tao album.
- Restore tu trash.
- Dung tren dien thoai.

Muc tieu khong phai public launch. Muc tieu la hoc xem san pham co dang tin, de dung va that su rieng tu hay khong.

## 2. Ai nen duoc moi

Nen moi:

- Nhom nho nguoi dung tin cay.
- Nguoi san sang bao bug.
- Nguoi hieu day la beta, tinh nang co the thay doi.
- Nguoi co nhu cau luu ky niem ca nhan that.

Chua nen:

- Mo cong khai.
- Dua len app store public ngay.
- Moi qua nhieu nguoi truoc khi storage/backup/monitoring san sang.

## 3. Checklist truoc private beta

- [ ] Real SMTP/email provider.
- [ ] Forgot password.
- [ ] Better logging.
- [ ] Backup database.
- [ ] Backup uploaded images.
- [ ] Storage quota policy.
- [ ] Thumbnail/compression.
- [ ] Basic rate limiting / lockout review.
- [ ] Better error pages.
- [ ] Privacy policy draft.
- [ ] Terms draft.
- [ ] Export data plan.
- [ ] Delete account plan.
- [ ] Mobile app prototype.
- [ ] Deployment environment.

## 4. Success criteria

Private beta co the xem la thanh cong neu:

- Users dang ky/dang nhap an toan.
- Users tao memories co anh ma khong gap loi nghiem trong.
- Users tin privacy model.
- Khong co cross-user data leaks.
- Khong co image upload/storage issue nghiem trong.
- Mobile UX cam thay tu nhien.
- Users hieu ro MemoLens khong phai social app.

## 5. Cau hoi feedback cho beta users

- Tao mot memory co du nhanh khong?
- Feeling list co de hieu khong?
- Viet note/story co tu nhien khong?
- Album organization co huu ich khong?
- Search/filter co giup tim lai ky niem khong?
- App co tao cam giac rieng tu khong?
- Co phan nao gay roi?
- Upload anh co cham hoac kho dung khong?
- Ban co lo ve privacy o diem nao khong?
- Ban muon export/delete data nhu the nao?

## 6. Gioi han cua beta

- Chua public app store.
- Storage gioi han.
- Support gioi han.
- Feature co the thay doi.
- Co the reset/migrate data neu can sua kien truc.
- Chua co AI.
- Chua co public sharing.

## 7. Cach van hanh beta an toan hon

- Moi theo tung dot nho.
- Ghi changelog ngan cho moi dot.
- Co kenh bao bug ro rang.
- Theo doi loi dang nhap, upload anh va image serving.
- Backup truoc khi deploy thay doi lon.
- Neu phat hien privacy leak, dung beta va fix truoc khi tiep tuc.
